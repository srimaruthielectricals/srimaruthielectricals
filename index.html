About Us

Sri Maruthi Electricals was established in 1987 with a vision of excellence in the electrical industry. Diligence, earnest commitment to meeting client project goals, and timely completion have contributed to securing ongoing projects. As the organization expanded, Sri Maruthi Electricals evolved to align with the demands of turnkey electrical projects. The board members, complemented by experienced managers, engineers, and technicians, oversee the company with a professional touch. The core philosophy of Sri Maruthi Electricals revolves around fostering enduring relationships with clients, achieved through the timely completion of projects and a steadfast commitment to quality. The company possesses the capability to undertake projects not only in Karnataka but also in various other states.
